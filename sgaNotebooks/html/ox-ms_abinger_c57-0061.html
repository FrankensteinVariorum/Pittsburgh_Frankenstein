I followed speedily I hardly knew
why but when the man saw me
draw near he aimed a gun which
he carried, at my body & fired. I
sunk to the ground & with encreased
swiftness He escaped into the wood.


This then was the reward of my
benevolence I
had saved a human
being from destruction and as a
recompence I now writhed under
the miserable pain of

a wound which
shattered the
flesh & bone &
The feelings
of kindness and gentleness which I had
entertained but a few moments before
gave place to hellish rage & gnashing
of teeth – inflamed by pain I vowed
eternal hatred & vengeance to all
mankind — But the agony of my wound overcame me
my pulses paused and I fainted.

life in these woods endeavouring to
