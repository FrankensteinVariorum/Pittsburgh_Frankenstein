The first part of this deposition did not
in the least interrest me but when the
mark of the fingers was mentioned I remem
bered the murder of my brother & felt
myself extremely agitated; my limbs trembled
& a mist came over my eyes which obliged
me to lean on a chair for support; the
Magistrate observed me with a keen eye
& of course drew an unfavourable augury from
my manner.


The son confirmed his fathers account
but when Daniel Nugent was questioned
he swore positively that just before the
fall of his companion he saw a boat with
a single man in it at a short distance
from the shore & as far as he could judge
by the light of a few stars it was the same
boat in which I had just landed.
