Such were the lessons of my father; but to
me the remembrance of the threat returned nor
can you wonder that omnipotent as the fiend
had yet been in his deeds of blood I should almost
regard him as invincible, and that when he had
pronounced the words, I shall be with you
on your wedding night, I should regard the
threatened fate as unavoidable. But death was
no evil to me if the loss of Elizabeth were balanced
with it and I therefore with a contented and even
cheerful countenance agreed with my father
that if my cousin would consent the ceremony
should take place in ten days; and thus put as I
imagined the seal to my fate.
