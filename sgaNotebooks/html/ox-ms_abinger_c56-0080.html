Yet as I drew nearer home grief and
fear overcame me – night also closed
round and when I could hardly
see the dark mountains I felt still
more gloomily I pictured every
evil and persuaded myself that
I was destined to become
the most wretched of human beings–
Alas! I prophesied truly and
failed onlyin one single circumstance: that in all the misery
I imagined and dreaded, I did not conceive
the hundredth part of the anguish

I was destined to endure.
