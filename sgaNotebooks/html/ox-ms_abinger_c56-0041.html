human nature turn from my

on by an eagerness which perpetually encreased,

I brought my work near to a conclusion.


The Summer months
passed while I was thus engaged,
heart and soul, in one pursuit. It
was a most beautiful season: never
did the fields bestow a more plentiful
harvest, or the vines yeild a more
luxuriant vintage. But my eyes were

insensible to the charms of nature & the
same feelings which made me
neglect the scenes around me caused
me also to forget those friends who
were so many miles absent and whom
I had not seen for so long a time.
