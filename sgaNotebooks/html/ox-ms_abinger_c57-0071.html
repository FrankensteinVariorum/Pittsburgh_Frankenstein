I consent to your demand on

I swear, he cried, by the sun & by
the blue sky of heaven that while they exist you
shall never behold me. Depart
then to your home & commence
your labours. I shall watch
their progress with unutterable
anxiety and fear not but that when
you are ready for me but that I shall appear.


Saying this he suddenly quitted me
fearful perhaps of any change in
my sentiments. I saw him descend
the mountain with greater speed than
the flight of the eagle and quickly lost
him among the undulations of the
sea of ice.


His tale had occupied the whole day
and the sun was upon the verge
