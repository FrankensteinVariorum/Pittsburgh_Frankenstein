Margaret, what comment can I make on the
untimely extinction of this glorious sprit? What
can I say that will make you understand
the depth of my sorrow? All that I should ex
-press would be inadequate & feeble. My tears
flow- My life is overshadowed with a cloud
of dissappointment. But I journey towards
England, & I may there find consolation.


I am interrupted— what do these sounds
portend? It is midnight. The breeze blows
fairly; & the watch on deck scarcely stir—
Again there is a sound as of a human voice
but hoarser- it comes from the cabin where the
remains of Frankenstein still lie. I must
arise & examine— Good night my sister- –
