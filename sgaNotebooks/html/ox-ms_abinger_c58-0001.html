Do not let this letter disturb you; do not
answer it tomorrow or the next day or even untill
you come if it will give you pain. My uncle will
send me news of your health and if I see but
one smile on your lips when we meet occasioned
by this or any other exertion of mine I
shall need no other happiness;
ElizabethLavenza
Geneva
May 18th17——
