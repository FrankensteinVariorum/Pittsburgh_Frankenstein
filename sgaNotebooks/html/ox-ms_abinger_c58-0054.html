fellow-feeling in my misery. No sympathy
may I ever find. When I first sought it, it
was the love of virtue. I desired that the feelings
of happiness & affection with the hope of which
my whole "being" once overflowed, should be par
-ticipated – But now, that virtue has become
to me a shadow, & happiness & affection are
turned into bitter & loathing despair, in what
should I seek for sympathy? I am contented
to suffer alone whilst my sufferings shall
endure. When I die, I am well satisfied
that abhorrence & opprobrium should load
my memory. Once my fancy was soothed with
dreams of virtue of fame & of enjoyment.
Once I falsely hoped to meet with beings
who pardoning my outward form would love me
