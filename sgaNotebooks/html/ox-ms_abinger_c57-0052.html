clared everlasting war against

him who had formed me & sent
me forth to this insupportable misery.


The sun rose – I heard the
voices of men and knew that it
was impossible to return to my retreat

during that day; accordingly, I hid myself
in some thick underwood
determining to devote the ensueing
hours to reflection on my situation—
