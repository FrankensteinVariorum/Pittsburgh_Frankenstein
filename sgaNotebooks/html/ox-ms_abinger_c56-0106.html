Thus the poor sufferer tried
to comfort others and herself. She indeed
gained the resignation she wished for
but I the true murderer felt
the never dying worm alive which
allowed no hope or consolation. Eliza
beth also wept and was unhappy but

which like a cloud that passes over the
fair moon & for a while

anguish and
