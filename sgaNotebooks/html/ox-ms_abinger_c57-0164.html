I exchanged my land sledge for
one fashioned for the ruggedness of the

frozen ocean and purchasing a plentiful stock
of provisions I departed from land. How
many days have passed since then I know
not but I have endured misery which
nothing but the eternal sentiment

of a just revenge burning within my heart could have enabled
me to support. Immense & rugged moun
