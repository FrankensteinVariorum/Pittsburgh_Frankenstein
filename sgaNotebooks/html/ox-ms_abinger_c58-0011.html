The sun sunk lower in the heavens; we
passed the river Drance and observed its path
through the chasms of the higher and the glens
of the lower hills. The Alps here come closer
to the lake and we approached the amphi-
theatre of mountains which forms the
eastern boundary of the lake. The spire of Evian
shone under the woods that surrounded
it and the range of mountain above mountain
by which it was overhung.
