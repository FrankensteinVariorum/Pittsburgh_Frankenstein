I was at first touched by the expression of his misery,
yet when I called to mind what Frankenstein
had said of his powers of eloquence & persua
-sion, & when I again cast my eyes on the
lifeless form of my friend, indignation was
re- kindled within me. — Wretch, I said,
it is well that you come here to whine over
the desolation which you have made – you
throw a torch into a pile of buildings, &
when they are consumed you sit among the
ruins & lament the fall. Hypocritical
fiend! If he whom you lament still lived,
still would he be the object, again would he
become the prey of your accursed vengeamce.
It is not pity that you feel– you lament
only because the victim of your malignity
is withdrawn from your power.——
