Cursed, Cursed Creator! Why
did I live why in that instant
did I not extinguish the spark of
existence which you had so wantonly
bestowed? I know not— Despair had not
yet taken possession of me
my feelings were those
of rage & revenge –I could with plea
sure have destroyed the cottage
and its inhabitants, and glutted my
self with their shrieks & misery.
When night came on I quitted
my retreat and wandered to the
wood.— No longer restrained
by the fear of discovery I gave vent
to my anguish in fearful howlings.
I was like a wild beast in the toils;

destroying the objects that obstructed
me and ranging through the
wood with stag like swiftness.—
Oh! What a miserable night I passed.
the cold stars shone in mockery,
the bare trees waved their branches
above me and now and then the sweet
voice of a bird burst forth anidst
the universal stillness.– All save I were
at rest or in enjoyment
I like the arch fiend bore a hell
within me and, finding my
self unsympathized with, I wished
