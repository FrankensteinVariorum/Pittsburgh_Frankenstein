Ever since my recovery from the fever
I had been in the custom of taking every
night a small quantity of laudanum
for it was by means of this drug only
that I was enabled to gain the rest necessa
ry for the preservation of life. Opressed
by the recollection of my various misfor
tunes I now took a double dose & soon
slept profoundly. But alas sleep did
not afford me respite from thought &
misery – my dreams presented
a thousand objects that scared me–
towards morning I was possessed by
